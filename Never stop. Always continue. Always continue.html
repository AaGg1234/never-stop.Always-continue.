Dog training tips

Potty training

  • Have a fix schedule
  • Take your dog out as soon as it eats and wakes up.
  • Reward your friend when it does on the needed place
  • Every dog has a different learning time so be patient while training

Teaching your dog to fetch

  • Use positive reinforcement
  • Use praises and rewards with pats on the back when it obeys you
  • Slowly stop treats and and continue with praises and pats on the back

Other tips

  • Be paitient as it takes time for little pups to get trained
  • Use rewards, praises and pats with positive reinforcement
  • This is the easiest way to train your best friend!!
